Calculation Explanation:

1. Yearly Statistics:
   - Calculate the average monthly temperature for each year.
   - Categorize years as Warm, Cold, or Normal based on quantiles (25th and 75th percentiles).

2. Monthly Statistics by Category:
   - Group data by Category (Warm, Cold, Normal) and Month.
   - Calculate the average monthly temperature for each category and month.

3. Most Warm and Cold Year Data:
   - Identify the most warm and cold years based on category.
   - Extract the monthly data for the most warm and cold years.
   - Create an Excel file with separate sheets for each type of data.
